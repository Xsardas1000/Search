OSAMU IYAMA, KIRIKO KATO AND JUN-ICHI MIYACHI

Abstract. We study a structure of subcategories which are called a poly-
gon of recollements in a triangulated category. First, we study a 2n-gon of
recollements in an (m/n)-Calabi-Yau triangulated category. Second, we show
the homotopy category K(MorNâˆ’1(B)) of complexes of an additive category
MorNâˆ’1(B) of N âˆ’1 sequences of split monomorphisms of an additive category
B has a 2N -gon of recollments. Third, we show the homotopy category KN (B)
of N -complexes of B has also a 2N -gon of recollments. Finally, we show there
is a triangle equivalence between K(MorNâˆ’1(B)) and KN (B).

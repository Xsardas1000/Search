THE ZIEGLER SPECTRUM FOR DERIVED-DISCRETE ALGEBRAS

KRISTIN KROGH ARNESEN, ROSANNA LAKING, DAVID PAUKSZTELLO, AND MIKE PREST

Abstract. Let Î› be a derived-discrete algebra. We show that the Krull-Gabriel di-
mension of the homotopy category of projective Î›-modules, and therefore the Cantor-
Bendixson rank of its Ziegler spectrum, is 2, thus extending a result of BobiÂ´nski and
Krause [9]. We also describe all the indecomposable pure-injective complexes and hence
the Ziegler spectrum for derived-discrete algebras, extending a result of Z. Han [16].
Using this, we are able to prove that all indecomposable complexes in the homotopy
category of projective Î›-modules are pure-injective, so obtaining a class of algebras
